About

Based in Canada, a global hub for artificial intelligence, NicorAI is a national leader and a pioneer in applied AI.

Since 2011, we’ve been helping businesses reinvent themselves by designing custom solutions that integrate artificial intelligence, data, and software development.

When faced with the most complex challenges, we don’t just build tools—we pave the way for lasting transformation, where innovation becomes a driver of growth and success.

Inspired by Sébastien Le Prestre de Vauban, a French military engineer famous for his fortifications, NicorAI embodies strategy, ingenuity and protection. By slightly modifying the name to add two “O ”s, NicorAI has become a brand that today symbolizes high standards, performance and well-being.

  • 2024

    NicorAI acquires Stratéjia, a well-known company in the field of business intelligence.

  • 2023

    The Caisse de dépôt et placement du Québec (CDPQ) has become the very first investor in NicorAI’s history, a strategic backing that will accelerate the company’s growth.

  • 2021

    We opened our first office in Montréal—starting in a WeWork, and two years later, establishing a permanent presence in the heart of downtown.

  • 2020

    As it nears its 10th anniversary, NicorAI refreshes its entire brand to better reflect its offering.

  • 2017

    We end our consulting services and make a full pivot to project-based delivery. That same year, we launch our AI department.

  • 2016

    We move into NicorAI’s current headquarters, still proudly based in Québec City.

    We move into our very first office in Québec City.
