Generative AI

Let’s unlock the potential of this technology in your business, together.

  • 01

    Step 1

    Training on Generative AI

    We offer a two half-day training on the fundamentals and applications of generative AI, designed for your teams and stakeholders, to demystify how it works and clarify its potential impact.

  • 02

    Step 2

    Workshops

    Through two collaborative workshops with your stakeholders, we identify relevant use cases for your business. These sessions are designed to uncover opportunities where generative AI can have the greatest impact.

  • 03

    Step 3

    Proof of Concept (PoC)

    Following the workshops, we develop a concrete proof of concept for the selected project. This PoC allows you to experiment with generative AI in a controlled environment and evaluate its return on investment.
